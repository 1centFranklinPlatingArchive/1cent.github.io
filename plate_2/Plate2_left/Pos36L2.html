1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 2
Position 36L2, (Scott #20 shown)
Blue,Type II, Relief A
Issued both imperforate and perforated.
Perforated copies are more scarce.

Courtesy of Marc Bedrin.
Vertical strip of 3, 26L2, 36L2, and 46L2.
Figure 1. (Below) Vertical line of color in center right margin as illustrated in the Neinken/Ashbrook plating diagram.

Figure 2. (Below) Ornament H has some weak places that should be noted. The dot at center left of Ornament H is very small and weak. The bottom curved line of Ornament H is also weak or broken. Just below this, there is a void to the right of the reverse-C ornament between H/K. While not unique to a particular position, these traits are very helpful in plating.
