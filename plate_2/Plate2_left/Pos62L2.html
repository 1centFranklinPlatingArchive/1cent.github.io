1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 2
Pos 62L2, (Scott #20)
Scott #20 Blue,
Type II, Relief B
Issued both imperforate and perforated.
Perforated copies are more scarce.
Figure 1. (Below) Detail view of dots located to the left of Ornament A. The upper arrow indicates a dot which is not illustrated on the plating diagram. It is not certain if this mark is constant or reliable.

Figure 2. (Below) Detail view of plating mark located in "O" of "ONE" as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagram. The diagonal blur to the left of the scroll is not illustrated in the plating diagram but is confirmed as consistent here.

Figure 3. (Below) Detail view of GUIDE DOT and plating marks located in "N" of "CENT" as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagram. The violet arrow indicates a strong dot which is not illustrated on plating diagrams. It is not certain if this mark is constant or reliable.
