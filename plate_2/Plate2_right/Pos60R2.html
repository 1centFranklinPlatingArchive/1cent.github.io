1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 2
Position 60R2
Blue,
Relief A, Type II
Issued both imperforate and perforated.
Figure 1. (Below)

PLATING NOTES:
Vertical scribe line in the right margin. Vertical dash of color to the right of Franklins nose in right margin past vertical scribe line. Neinken indicates a plating mark above Ornament Z1.

Figure 2. (Below) Image Courtesy of the PSE.
Other marks of interest, Small DASH between the tops of Ormanents S and T. Small blur DOT of color in the P of POSTAGE.
