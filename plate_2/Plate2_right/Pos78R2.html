1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 2
Position 78R2, (Scott #7 shown)
Blue, Type II, Relief A
Issued both imperforate (Scott 7) and perforated (Scott 20).

Part of a horizontal strip of 3 consisting of 78R2, 79R2 and 80R2.
Courtesy of Tyler Moody

PLATING NOTES:
Neinken indicates that this position has no plating marks. It should be noted that the GUIDE DOT at the upper right is technically part of 68R2. The plating diagram for 68R2 shows a diagonal line extending from that GUIDE DOT which we can see easily on this 78R2 patient.

Figure 1. (Below) Detail view of a dot of color and vertical blur above Ornament M not illustrated in the Neinken plating diagram but confirmed as consistent.

Figure 2. (Below) Detail view of a vertical line of color in the O of POSTAGE not illustrated in the Neinken plating diagram but confirmed as consistent. It should be noted that similar plating marks like this one shown occur on several positions from Plate 2.
