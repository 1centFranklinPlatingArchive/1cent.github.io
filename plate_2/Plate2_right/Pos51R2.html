1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 2
Position 51R2, (Scott #7 shown)
Blue,
Relief A, Type II
Issued both imperforate and perforated.
Figure 1. (Below)

PLATING NOTES:
From a block of 70, Ex-Hind. The full sheet is shown in Neinken pg.189 Figure 13-T.

Plating marks in O, T and A of POSTAGE.
Dot is right scroll.
