1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 2
Pos 38R2, (Scott #20)
Scott #7 Blue,
Type II Relief A
Issued both imperforate (Scott7) and perforated (Scott 20).
Perforated copies are more scarce.

Violet arrows indicate marks of interest not shown on the plating diagrams and are not known if constant or reliable.
Figure 1. Detail view of plating marks found the U of US.

Figure 2. (Below) Detail view of the plating marks in A and G of POSTAGE. The guide dot is part of Position 28R2. The violet arrows are marks of note that are not shown in the Ashbrook/Neinken plating diagrams.

Figure 3. (Below) Detail view of the plating marks in O and E of ONE.
