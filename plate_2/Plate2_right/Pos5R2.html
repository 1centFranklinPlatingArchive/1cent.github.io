1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 2
Pos 5R2, (Scott #7)
Scott #7 Blue
Type II, Relief T,
Issued both imperforate and perforated.
Perforated copies are more scarce.

NEW PLATING MARK, See figure 3 below.
Figure 1. (Below) Detail view of PLATE FLAW above the "P" in POSTAGE. Worn impressions of this mark appear as a blur.

Figure 2. (Below) Detail view of the upper right corner showing the GUIDE DOT and a small companion.

Figure 3. (Below) New plating marks discovered by the Archivist. A diagonal mark of color can be seen to the right and below Ornament M. Two faint vertical lines not indicated on the plating diagrams.
