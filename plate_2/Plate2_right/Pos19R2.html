1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 2
Position 19R2, (Scott #7 shown)
Scott #7, Blue,
Type II, Relief A,
Issued both imperforate and perforated.
Perforated copies are more scarce.

Part of a strip of 6 consisting of 15R2-20R2
Courtesy of Stanley M. Piller and Associates.
Figure 1. (Below) The far right arrow points to a scratch or line line of color as indicated in the plating diagrams.

Figure 2. (Below) The GUIDE DOT at the upper right corner. All positions of the 9th vertical row of the right pane have either single or double dots to the right of Ornament Z.

Figure 3. (Below) A GUIDE DOT at the extreme lower right corner is technically belonging to Position 29R2.

Figure 4. (Below) Some stamps of this position will show a dot in the O of ONE but it appears to not be consistent.
