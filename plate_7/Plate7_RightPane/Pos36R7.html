1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-61, PLATE 7.
Position 36R7, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief D
Issued only perforated.

This is part of a horizontal strip of 4 consisting of 34R, 35R, 36R and 37R7.
