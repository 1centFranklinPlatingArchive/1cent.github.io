1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-61, PLATE 7.
Pos 78R7, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief D
Issued only perforated.

Dot under "O" of ONE.

This position has many more plating marks which are obscured by cancellation ink. The impression and paper is very soft and contributes to the inability of the fine lines found on this position from showing up clearly.
Figure 1. (Below) Plating marks as shown in the Neinken plating diagrams. The dot under "O" of ONE and a vertical line of color in the left plume remains. The violet arrow is a blur of color which can be an additional aid to plating.

Figure 2. (Below) Plating mark east of Ornament "O."

Figure 3. (Below) The red arrows indicate blurs of color in the letters "S. POS" of US POSTAGE. These are shown in the plating diagrams.
