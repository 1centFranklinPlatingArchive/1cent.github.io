1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-61, PLATE 7.
Position 14L7, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief B
Issued only perforated.

Horizontal pair consisting of 4L7 and 14L7.

Figure 1. (Below) The plating diagram illustrates a dot of color next to the small unnamed ornament inside of Ornament H. Neinken indicates this dot on many B relief positions from Plate 7. Additionally, the heavy dot in the label border to the left of the O of ONE, not illustrated in the plating mat, has been confirmed as constant.

Figure 2. (Below) A blur and diagonal line of color below Ornament M, not illustrated in the plating mat, can be found on early or sharp impressions but appears to fade or disappear completely in weak or worn impressions.

Figure 3. (Below) A blur and jagged horizontal line of color above the S of U.S., not illustrated in the plating mat, can be found on early or sharp impressions but appears to fade or disappear completely in weak or worn impressions.
