1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-61, PLATE 7.
Position 17L7, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief B
Issued only perforated.

NOTES: The Neinken plating diagram illustrates a dot in the T of POSTAGE. The archive has 4 images of this position and the dot does not appear on any of those examples.

Courtesy of Mark and Sandy Friedman

Figure 1. (Below) A nearly vertical line of color to the right of Ornament E and a blurry dash of color above Ornament E as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagram. Additionally, there is a dot of color in the should which appears to change intensity depending on clarity of impression. This mark on the shoulder is not illustrated in the plating diagram.

Figure 2. (Below) Neinken illustrates a strong dot to the left of the unnamed small ornament inside of Ornament H, but does not indicate the small dot to the right, which is found on many other B reliefs from Plate 7. Also not illustrated but confirmed here as consistent are several plating marks in the O of ONE.
