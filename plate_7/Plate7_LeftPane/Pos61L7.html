1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-61, PLATE 7.
Pos 61L7, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief C
Issued only perforated.

This stamp below is on cover and and is a poor impression Many of the plating marks shown on the Neinken mats are obscured by cancellation ink. See updated images Figures 2 and 3.
