1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 9
Position 64R9, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief C
Issued only Perforate.

This position is not illustrated in the Neinken plating diagrams. Marks of interest are shown in detail in an attempt to identify the position with certainty if one only possesses a single stamp. The image below is from a block of four consisting of 63R9, 64R9, 73R9 and 74R9.
