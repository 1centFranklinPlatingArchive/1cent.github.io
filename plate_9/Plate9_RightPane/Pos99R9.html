1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 9
Position 99R9, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief F
Issued only Perforate.

This position is not illustrated in the Neinken plating diagrams and is noted as not having plating marks. Almost every position from Plate 9 has some identifing feature and careful examination of multiple patients will most likely reveal some consistent plating mark.

Courtesy of David Mason

FIGURE 1. (Below) A heavy vertical dash of color and a strong dot to the right of Ornament A/B confirmed as consistent. See archive Position 98R9 for a confirming view of this plating mark. The heavy mark at the top of the P of POSTAGE has been confirmed as consistent.

FIGURE 2. (Below) A blurry diagonal line of color protruding from Ornament R confirmed as consistent.
