1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 9
Pos 62R9, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief C
Issued only Perforate.

DASH IN HEAD VARIETY

This position is not illustrated in the Neinken plating diagrams. Marks of interest are shown in detail in an attempt to differentiate the 10 "dash in head" positions, 28-29-30L, 68-69-70L, 21-22R and 61-62R. The top edge of this stamp displays parts of the big double transfer on 52R9.
FIGURE 1. (Below) Six positions of the left pane and four positions in the right pane produced the variety called the "DASH IN HEAD". A small particle got on the "C" relief after the entry of 63R, as this position does not show it. It first appears on position 22R and hung to the the relief roller for the next ten entries, disappearing after 68L9 was entered. These positions are 28-29-30L, 68-69-70L, 21-22R and 61-62R.
