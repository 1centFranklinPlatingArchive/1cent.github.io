1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 9
Position 76R9, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief D
Issued only Perforate.

It is interesting to note that the D reliefs coming from the 8th horizontal row (71R9 thru 80R9 and 77L9 thru 80L9) have blobs of color in the O and T of POSTAGE. Neinken notes this only on 72R9, 78R9, 80R9 and 80L9 but not the other positions.

Courtesy of Mark and Sandy Friedman.
FIGURE 1. (Below) Heavy dots of color in the lower left ornaments and the N of ONE as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagrams.

FIGURE 2. (Below) Dots of color to the right of Ornament R as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagrams. Other marks of interest in the lower right ornaments and E of CENT have not been confirmed as consistent.

FIGURE 3. (Below) Right pane D reliefs coming from the 8th horizontal row (71R thru 80R) have blobs of color in the O and T of POSTAGE. 76R9 also features a nick of color in the loop of P and a blurry dot of color above Ornament S. Neinken notes similar plating marks only on 72R9, 78R9 and 80R9.
