1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 9
Pos 58R9, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief F
Issued only Perforate.

Note that Neinkens plating diagrams indicate that 53, 57, 58 and 59R9 have no plating marks. This is an error in the case of Position 58R. There are many plating marks as we will show below. An attempt to show the most obvious marks of note are indicated here. Several other students of this issue have emailed confirmation of this position as being 58R9 and as this is part of a pair, we have final confirmation of it's position on the plate.

This is part of a pair consisting of 58 and 59R9. Be sure to examine a later impression of 58R9
FIGURE 1. (Below) Faint gash mark thru the "G" in POSTAGE. This mark and position placement has been confirmed on 2 other copies.

FIGURE 2. (Below) Dick Celler, another student of this issue, emails the following confirmation. "... There is also a very heavy mark in the top of the vertical stroke of the "P" of POSTAGE. (Red Arrow)
