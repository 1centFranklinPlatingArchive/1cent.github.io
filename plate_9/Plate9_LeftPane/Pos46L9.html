1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 9
Pos 46L9, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief E
Issued only Perforate.

The most prominent feature of Plate 9 is the fine plate scratch lines found in various places on both sides of the panes. These lines are somewhat similar to the polishing scratch marks found on Plate 8, but on Plate 9 they are not straight in many parts of the plate, and from the different directions in which they run, they have the appearance of surface cracks. Such, however was not their origin.

The red arrow to the lower left is a plating mark found to the left of Ornament "G" on 47L9
