1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-61, PLATE 5.

Pos 32R5, (Scott #24 var)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type Va, Relief D
Issued only perforated.
Figure 1. (Below) The Neinken plating diagrams illustrate very faint plate scratches in the lower right plume remains. On this worn impression scratches cannot be seen. The remains of a blur, including a strong constant dot, can be seen to the right of Ornament R.

Figure 2. (Below) The plating diagrams illustrate a small dot inside of Ornament F/G and a diagonal dash of color below the N of ONE. The violet arrow indicates a small dot which has been confirmed. There is also a small diagonal dash of color inside of Ornament G which has been confirmed as constant.

Figure 3. (Below) The location of the large blur as illustrated in the plating diagram.
