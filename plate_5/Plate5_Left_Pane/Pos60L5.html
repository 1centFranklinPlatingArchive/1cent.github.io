1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-61, PLATE 5
Position 60L5 (Scott #24 var)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type Va, Relief F
Issued only perforated.

Part of a vertical pair 50L5 and 60L5.

Figure 1. (Below) The plating diagram illustrates many plating marks and faint plate scratches at the right side. See Figure 5. for an earlier impression.
Figure 2. (Below) Detail view of the plate scratches in the upper left ornaments as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagram. Additionally, there are two dots of color to the left and just below Ornament C not illustrated in the plating diagram but appear to be consistent.

Figure 3. (Below) Detail view of the plate scratches in the upper right ornaments as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagrams.

Figure 4. (Below) Detail of the lower right corner showing heavy blurs of color below right ball and plume. The plating diagram does not quite illustrate this feature clearly.

Figure 5. (Below) Courtesy of SunCoast Stamps Florida.
An earlier impression showing numerous plate scratches as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagrams.
