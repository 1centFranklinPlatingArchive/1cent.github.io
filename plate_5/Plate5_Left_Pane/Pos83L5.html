1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-61, PLATE 5.
Position 83L5, (Scott #24 )
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief E
Issued only perforated.

Part of a vertical pair 73L5 and 83L5

PLATING NOTES:
The start of what appears to be a plate blister above the A and G of POSTAGE.
Horizontal blurs of color protruding from Ornaments O and Q.
Figure 1. (Below) Plating features in the upper left quadrant as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagram. Additional scratches and dots of color in Ornaments A/B not illustrated in the plating diagram.

Figure 2 (Below) Various plating marks in the upper right quadrant as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagrams. The start of what appears to be a plate blister above the A and G of POSTAGE, a feature not illustrated in the plating diagram.

Figure 3 (Below) Various plate scratches in the lower left quadrant as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagrams.
