1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-61, PLATE 5
Position 57L5
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief F
Issued only perforated.

PLATING NOTES:
A vertical line of dots to the right of Ornaments O, P, Q and R.
Plating mark located in the O of ONE.
Vertical plate scratches in Ornaments H, H1. Blur of color in Ornament U.
