1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 1 LATE.

Pos 6L1L, (Scott #9)
Scott #9 Blue,
Type IV, Relief T
Issued both imperforate and perforated.
Perforated copys are more scarce.

DOUBLE RECUT BOTTOM CURVED LINE
Figure 1. (Below) Red arrows indicate plating marks in Ashbrook/Neinken drawings.

Figure 2. (Below) Red arrows indicate plating marks in Ashbrook/Neinken drawings. Violet arrows are marks not shown in plating drawings and may not be constant. Only positions 1, 2, 3, 6, 7, 9 and 10 of the LEFT pane have a DOUBLE RECUT bottom curved lines. This can be an aid to plating top row positions of this pane.
