1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 1 LATE.
Pos 51L1L, (Scott #9)
Scott #9 Blue,
Type IV, Relief A
Issued both imperforate and perforated.
Perforated copies are more scarce.

DOUBLE RECUT TOP and
SINGLE RECUT BOTTOM

Had this patient had real perforations, it would of been a Scott #23.

Figure 1. (BELOW). Detail view of TOP DOUBLE RECUT LINES (Yellow arrows) and plating marks (Red) The marks in the "P" of POSTAGE appear to be rust marks.

Figure 2. (BELOW). Detail view of upper left ornaments. Note the thick curved line of the medallion border.
