1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 1 LATE.
Pos 56R1L, (Scott #9)
Scott #9 Blue,
Type IV, Relief A
Issued both imperforate and perforated.
Perforated copies are more scarce.

SINGLE RECUT TOP
SINGLE RECUT BOTTOM
SPLIT TOP LINE

Part of a Strip of 3, Positions 54-55-56R1L
Figure 1. (BELOW) Detail view of TOP RECUT LINE. The recut line splits away from the original design, hence the SPLIT TOP LINE variety.

Figure 2. (BELOW). Detail of the BOTTOM recut line. The recut gently dips below the "E" of ONE.

Figure 3. (Below) The upper left as well as the other parts of the NW corner became slightly doubled during the reentry of this position, as illustrated in the Ashbrook diagrams. This is best seen on the small unnamed ornament between Ornaments A and S. (red arrow)
