1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 1 LATE
Position 19R1L
Scott #9 Blue,
Type IV, Relief A
Issued both imperforate and perforated.
Perforated copies are more scarce.

SINGLE RECUT TOP
SINGLE RECUT BOTTOM

Note that Ornament "S" is weakly impressed. This is not indicated on the Ashbrook plating diagrams. Plating marks of note are indicated below until a better impression is obtained.
Figure 1. (Below) Detail view of the top recut line. Note that the recut appears to flatten out above the "O and S" of POSTAGE.

Figure 2. (Below) Detail view of the bottom recut line and plating marks in the "O" of ONE. The Ashbrook plating diagrams also indicate other plating marks below the "N and T" of CENTS, but cannot be seen on this copy.

Figure 3. (Below) Plating marks in the upper right quadrant per the Ashbrook diagrams.
