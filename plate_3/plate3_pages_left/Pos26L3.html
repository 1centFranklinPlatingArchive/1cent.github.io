1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 3
Position 26L3, (Scott #7)
Scott #7 Blue,
Type II, Relief A
Issued only imperforate.

Part of a horizontal strip of 3 consisting of 24L3, 25L3 and 26L3.

PLATING NOTES:
This strip is significant because 24L3 shows part of the "forked lightning crack", 25L3 as shown in the Neinken plating diagram indicates as having no plating marks or GUIDE DOT, and 26L3 of which the plating diagram illustrated is incorrect. Note guide dot at lower right.
