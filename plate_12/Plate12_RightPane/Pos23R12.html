1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1861, PLATE 12
Pos 23R12 (Scott #18)
Scott #18 Blue,
Type I, Relief C
Only Issued perforate

The violet arrows are marks of interest not shown on the plating diagrams. The lower part of the stamp clearly shows the complete plumes and scroll balls indicative of the Type I.
