1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 1 EARLY
Position 35L1E, (Scott #7)
Scott #7 Blue,
Type II, Relief A First Condition
Only Issued Imperforate

THIS PATIENT IS PART OF A HORIZONTAL STRIP OF 3
Consisting of 34L, 35L and 36L1E
Figure 1. Red arrows show vertical lines which are shown in Ashbrook/Neinken plating drawings. It should be noted that the plating drawings only show 2 of the 3 lines. This may be due to the quality of the impression or that the lines wore away with the plate wearing down.The violet arrows below also point out lines not shown on the plating diagrams.
