1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 1 EARLY
Position 17L1E, (Scott #7)
Scott #7 Blue,
Type II, Relief A First Condition
Only Issued Imperforate

This image is part of a reconstructed strip of 3 consisting of 15L1E, 16L1E and 17L1E.

PLATING NOTES: Note that Ornament P and the small unnamed ornament below it is weak. This is shown in the plating diagrams but not detailed as a plating aid.
