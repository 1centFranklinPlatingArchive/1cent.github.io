1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 10
Position 10R10, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief A
Issued only Perforate.
Part of a vertical strip, consisting of 10R10, 20R10, 30R10, 40R10 and 50R10

PLATING NOTES:
Ornament F is complete, This feature is unusual on a plate 10 top row. 9L10 and 9R10 are also complete.
