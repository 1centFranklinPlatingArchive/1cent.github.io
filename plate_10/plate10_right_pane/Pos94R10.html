1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 10
Position 94R10, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief F
Issued only Perforate.

The Neinken plating diagram does not illustrate this position.
All marks indicated below are unconfirmed. Confirming copies need to be obtained to verify consistency.

Part of a horizontal strip consisting of 94R10, 95R10 and 96R10

The heavy dot in the "C" of CENT has been confirmed as constant and also has been confirmed on Positions 91 thru 100R10. The plating diagrams do not indicate this mark. This dot was probably a bit of matter that attached itself to the transfer roller when the plate was being made and should also appear on 6th row positions (51R thru 60R).
