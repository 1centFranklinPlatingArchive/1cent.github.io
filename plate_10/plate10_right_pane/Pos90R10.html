1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 10
Position 90R10, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief E
Issued only Perforate.
Part of a Block of 4 consisting of 89R10, 90R10, 99R10 and 100R10.

The violet arrows are marks of interest and may or may not be constant. Red arrows are known plating marks indicated by the Ashbrook / Neinken plating diagrams. Other features to note are the numerous rust marks in the right margin.
FIGURE 1. (Below) Faint plating marks per plating diagrams.

FIGURE 2. (Below) Faint vertical line per plating diagrams.
Note the rust marks in the right margin. These can be an aid to plating.
