1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 10
Position 37R10, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief D
Issued only Perforate.

Part of a block of four consisting of 26R10, 27R10, 36R10 and 37R10
FIGURE 1. (Below) The Neinken plating diagram illustrates a plating mark in the E and below the C of CENT. The archives 3 blocks do not show this plating mark but does reveal heavy blurs below the label.

FIGURE 2. (Below) The Neinken plating diagram does not illustrate this diagonal dash of color inside of Ornament P/Q but is confirmed as consistent here.

FIGURE 3. (Below) The Neinken plating diagram illustrates plating marks in Ornament H and at the lower part of Ornament K. They are faint and inconsistent.
