1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 10
Pos 93L10, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief E
Issued only Perforate.

Courtesy of Dick Celler

Part of a horizontal pair 92L10, 93L10
FIGURE 1. (Below) Plating marks in upper left ornaments as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagram.

FIGURE 2. (Below) Blurry vertical line of color in Ornament R as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagram.

FIGURE 3. (Below) The heavy dot in the "C" of CENT has been confirmed as constant and also has been confirmed on Positions 91 thru 100R10. The plating diagrams do not indicate this mark. This dot was probably a bit of matter that attached itself to the transfer roller when the plate was being made.
