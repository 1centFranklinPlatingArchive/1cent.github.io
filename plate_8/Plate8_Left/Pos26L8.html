1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 8
Position 26L8, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief C
Issued only Perforate.

Part of an unused block of 6 consisting of 26L, 27L, 28L, 36L, 37L and 38L8
This position is not illustrated in the Neinken plating diagrams and is noted as not having plating marks. The archivist disagrees with Neinkens' assumption given that almost every position from Plate 8 has some identifing feature. At this point in time the archive has no confirming position but now that we have an image it should be a matter of time before confirmed plating marks can be found.
