1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 8
Pos 32L8, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief D
Issued only Perforate.

FIGURE 1. (Below) Vertical lines of color to the left of Ornaments C and D as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagram. There is also a small dot of color to the left of Ornament B not illustrated in the diagrams but confirmed here as consistent.

FIGURE 2. (Below) Vertical lines of color in the center left margin as illustrated in the Neinken plating diagram. There is also a small blur of color to the left of the D Relief side scratch not illustrated in the diagrams but confirmed here as consistent.
