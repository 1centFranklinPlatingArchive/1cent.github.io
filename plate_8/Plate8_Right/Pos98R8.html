1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 8
Pos 98R8, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief F
Issued only Perforate.

Part of a Strip of 3 on cover. 98R8, 99R8 and 100R8.
