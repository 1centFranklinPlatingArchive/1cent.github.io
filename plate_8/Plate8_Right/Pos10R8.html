1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 8
Pos 10R8, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief A
Issued only Perforate.

DOUBLE TRANSFER

Unlike Plates 5 and 7, neither of which produced any double transfers, Plate 8 had two such varieties, Positions 8R8 and 10R8, both occurring in the top row of the right pane. These two double transfers prove that the two small vertical scribe lines at the NW corner were part of and on the "A" relief part of the relief transfer roller because the double transfers of both positions are shown in the doubling of the small vertical lines as well as the stamp design itself.

Stamps from Positions 4R8 thru 10R8 also show a heavy dot on the shoulder above the "N" of ONE. This dot occurs in the same place on these seven positions and readily identifies such stamps as coming from the right pane of Plate 8. On this copy it is obscured by cancellation ink.
