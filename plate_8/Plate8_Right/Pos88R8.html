1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 8
Pos 88R8, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief E
Issued only Perforate.

DOT UNDER "S" VARIETY
The dot under S appears more to look like an apostrophe in reality.

Position 47R8 also shows the apostrophe under S. The plating diagrams do not differentiate these positions clearly. Several curls and dots not shown in the plating diagrams do help the student definitively plate these positions.
FIGURE 1. (Below) The "dot" or apostrophe plating mark as indicated in Ashbrook/Neinken plating diagrams.

FIGURE 2. (Below) Plating diagrams do not show the dots below O and N of ONE. The red arrows are confirmed as consistent.

FIGURE 2. (Below) Plating diagrams do not show the faint curl in the top right ornament. This curl has been confirmed as consistent and shows inself on Position 47R8 and 48R8.
