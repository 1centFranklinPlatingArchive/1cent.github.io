1¢ Franklin Issue of 1857-1861, PLATE 8
Pos 20R8, (Scott #24)
Scott #24 Blue,
Type V, Relief B
Issued only Perforate.

Part of a vertical pair consisting of 20R8 and 30R8.
FIGURE 1. (Below) Faint horizontal lines of color on 2 different impressions. These are not shown in the Neinken plating diagrams.

FIGURE 1. (Below) Faint horizontal lines of color cutting across the O of ONE on 2 different impressions. These are not shown in the Neinken plating diagrams.
