1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 4
Position 15L4

Type III, Relief B
Issued both imperforate (Scott 8) and perforate (Scott 21).

Plating Notes: No high resolution image is available at this time. Neinken illustrates this position on page 270, Fig 15-N. Long horizontal scratch can be seen in the head and on into the right margin protruding out from between Ornaments M/N. The near vertical blurry line of color protruding from Ornament S on the right most stamp (16L4) can be seen below. The broken bottom curved line under the CE of CENT is clearly visible and is the principal feature (along with the broken top line) of a Type III stamp.
