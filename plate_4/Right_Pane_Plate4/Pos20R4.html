1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 4
Position 20R4

Blue, Type IIIA, Relief B, Transfer Roller Number 2.
Issued both imperforate (8A) and perforate (22).

PLATING NOTES: The plating of positions 20R4 and 30R4 from the Neinken book can be a challenge as many marks found on 20R4 are not noted in the plating diagram. There are several plating marks which identify this position. There is a dot on the shoulder at the far left side. There is a dot in the top of the ‘T’ of ‘POSTAGE’. There are several faint near-horizontal scratches in the head. There is a scratch touching ornament N. The bottom scrolls are very weak. Most importantly, there is a vertical guide line between ornaments L and M which is quite close to ornament L1.
