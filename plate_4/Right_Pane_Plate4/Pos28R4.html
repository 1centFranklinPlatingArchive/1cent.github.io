1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 4
Position 28R4
Blue, Type IIIA-III, Relief C, Transfer Roller Number 2.
Issued both imperforate (8A-8) and perforate (22-21).

PLATING NOTES: Neinken illustrates this position but marked the diagram with no plating marks. Closer examination shows a faint vertical line connecting Ornaments L and M. The line continues vertically next to Ornament Z1 and appears to end as a small dot. On left margined patients the deep plate scratch should be visible near Ornament C.

FIGURE 1. (BELOW) Digital Reconstruction of 27R4 and 28R4 showing the deep plate scratch protruding from Ornament N on 27R4 and connecting to Ornament C on 28R4.

Figure 1. (BELOW) Faint vertical line connecting Ornaments L and M. The line continues vertically next to Ornament Z1 and appears to end as a small dot. On left margined patients the deep plate scratch should be visible near Ornament C. It is not known if the dot of color between Ornaments A/B is consistent. Other blurs and marks in the margins should aid the plater.

Figure 2. (BELOW) The right half of the top curved line appears thicker than the left side.
