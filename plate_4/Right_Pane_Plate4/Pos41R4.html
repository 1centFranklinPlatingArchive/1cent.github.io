1¢ Franklin Issue of 1851-1857, PLATE 4
Position 41R4
Blue,
Type IIIa-Ic, Relief E
Issued both imperforate (8A-6b) and perforate (22-19b).

FIGURE 1. (Below) Image Courtesy of Mark Leon

Figure 2. (BELOW) Strong vertical plating mark in the N of CENT, faint line in the T of CENT and a large blurry DOT in the lower right plume. These marks are not illustrated in the plating diagram. The image at the left does show the BLUR plating mark in the leftside of the N of CENT as indicated "on early only" in the Neinken plating diagram.


Figure 3. (BELOW) Vertical plate scratches in lower left margin and in the N of ONE. The plating diagram for early impressions also illustrates a plate scratch in the E of ONE.


Figure 4. (BELOW) Deep horizontal plate scratch to the right of Ornament A as indicated in the revised Neinken plating diagram.
